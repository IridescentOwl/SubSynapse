# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

This policy outlines when you may be eligible for a refund for payments made through Subsynapse.

## 1. Payment Models

* **Temporary:** A one-time, upfront payment for a defined period.
* **Monthly:** A recurring, auto-debiting payment for ongoing access.

## 2. Eligible Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund in the following specific cases:

* **Group Dissolution by Owner:** If you are a paying Group Member and the Group Owner deletes the group, you may be eligible for a **prorated refund** for the unused portion of the billing cycle.

## 3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **NOT** be processed in the following situations:

* **Violation of Terms:** If your account is suspended or terminated for violating our Terms and Conditions (e.g., non-payment, misuse).
* **Third-Party Action:** If your access to a subscription is revoked by the third-party provider (e.g., Netflix) for any reason.
* **Change of Mind:** We do not offer refunds if you simply change your mind after a payment has been processed.

## 4. Non-Refundable Fees

* Please note that the **platform fees** and **payment processing fees** charged by Subsynapse are **non-refundable** in all circumstances. Any refund issued will be for the subscription cost *minus* these fees.

## 5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at **subsynapsecampus@gmail.com** with your name, transaction details, and the reason for your request. We will review each request on a case-by-case basis.
