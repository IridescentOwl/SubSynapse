# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

Welcome to Subsynapse (the "Platform"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

* **Personal Information:** Your full name, phone number, and email address (specifically your `@thapar.edu` email for verification).
* **Usage Data:** Information about your activity on the Platform, such as the subscriptions you share or join, your transaction history, and engagement metrics (e.g., time spent on the service).
* **Cookies:** We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our service. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how you use the Platform.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Operate and maintain the Platform.
* **Verify your identity** as a current student of Thapar University.
* **Facilitate "matchmaking"** between Group Owners and Group Members.
* Process your transactions (via our payment gateway).
* Communicate with you, including sending service updates and support messages.
* Monitor and analyze usage to improve the Platform.

## 3. Payment Processing

We use a third-party payment processor, **Razorpay**, to handle all financial transactions. We do **not** collect, store, or process your credit/debit card details or bank account information. Your payment information is provided directly to Razorpay, and its use is governed by Razorpay's own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review their policy.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

* **With Third-Party Service Providers:** To perform tasks on our behalf, such as payment processing (Razorpay), data analysis, and hosting services.
* **For Legal Reasons:** If required by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., a court order).
* **With Your Consent:** We may share your information for other purposes if we have your explicit consent.

*We reserve the right to share aggregated, non-personally identifiable usage data with partners or for marketing purposes.*

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that **no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure**. We cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We do **not** store or have access to the login credentials (username/password) for your third-party subscription accounts (e.g., your Netflix password).

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **subsynapsecampus@gmail.com**
